== Installation ==

1. The plugin will automatically display the coming soon banner
2. All of the options for the plugin are located under Apperance > Customize > Fancy Coming Soon Settings
3. Your mailchimp Signup URL can be found by following these directions: http://kb.mailchimp.com/lists/signup-forms/share-your-signup-form

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

1. To disable your coming soon banner, navigation to Plugins and de-activate your Fancy Coming Soon Plugin for WordPress.  This will disable the banner.
2. You can disable the theme options previewer under Apperance > Customize > Fancy Coming Soon Settings > General > Preview Coming Soon Page?
